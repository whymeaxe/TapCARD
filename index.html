How it works

Running in minutes,
not hours

01
Create your card
Pick a template or build from scratch. Add your details, photo, and links. Takes about three minutes.
02
Share anywhere
Tap via NFC, show a QR code, or send your unique link. No app required on the receiver's end.
03
Collect & organize
Scan paper cards or receive digital ones. Every contact is automatically categorized.
04
Track and follow up
See card views, get follow-up reminders, and sync everything to your CRM automatically.
